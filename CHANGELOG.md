# SoapsQuest - What's New

Welcome to SoapsQuest! Here's what's been added to make your questing experience even better.

## [1.0.2] - November 5, 2025

### New Features

**🔗 Quest Chains & Prerequisites**
- Some quests now require you to complete other quests first!
- Unlock special quests by completing a certain number of quests
- Server owners can create epic quest storylines with dependencies
- You'll see clear messages about which quests you need to complete first

**✨ Better Jump Quest Tracking**
- Jump quests now count correctly - each jump counts as one, no matter how high you jump
- No more accidental extra progress from jumping up hills
- More accurate tracking means fair challenges for everyone

**📊 Improved Server Logging**
- Server owners can now track quest activity better (doesn't affect your gameplay)
- Helps servers monitor popular quests and player progress

---

## [1.0.1] - November 1, 2025

### New Features

**💾 Save Your Favorite Randomly Generated Quests**
- Love a random quest? Now you can save it permanently!
- Server admins can use `/sq save <quest_id>` to keep quests from being deleted
- Use `/sq transfer <quest_id>` to make a random quest permanent
- Old, abandoned quests get cleaned up automatically (your progress is always safe!)
- See who created each quest and when it was made

**🎁 Better Custom Item Rewards**
- Custom items from popular plugins now work perfectly in quest rewards
- Supports EcoItems, MMOItems, Oraxen, and ItemsAdder
- Server owners can easily add custom weapons and tools as quest rewards
- Cleaner quest files behind the scenes

**🎨 Customize Quest Display Names**
- Quest names can now show tier colors and badges
- More control over how quest names look in your inventory
- Make your quests look unique and professional

## [1.0.0] - October 28, 2025

### Welcome to SoapsQuest! 🎉

**What is SoapsQuest?**
SoapsQuest brings immersive questing to your Minecraft server with physical quest papers you can hold in your inventory!

**Main Features:**

📜 **Physical Quest Papers**
- Hold your quests in your hand as actual items
- Track your progress in real-time
- Complete objectives and claim rewards

🎯 **33+ Quest Types**
- Mining, building, crafting, combat, fishing, and more
- Complete multiple objectives in one quest
- Daily and weekly recurring quests

⭐ **Tier & Rarity System**
- 8 different quest tiers from Common to Legendary
- Harder quests give better rewards
- Feel the excitement of finding rare quests

🎁 **Amazing Rewards**
- Earn XP, money, and items
- Unlock special commands when you complete quests
- Chain quests together for epic storylines

🎲 **Endless Variety**
- Quests are randomly generated for unlimited content
- Always something new to do
- Never run out of challenges

📊 **Track Your Progress**
- See your stats and achievements
- Compete on server leaderboards
- Get notified when you reach milestones

🎨 **Beautiful Interfaces**
- Easy-to-use quest menus
- Colorful quest papers
- Clean and intuitive design

💎 **Special Features**
- Get quest papers from mob drops
- Personal quests locked just for you
- Work on multiple quests at once
- Multi-language support
