SoapsQuest V1.0.1 Changelog
============================

Release date: May 2026

Overview
--------
Bug fixes, tracking improvements, documentation updates, and clearer Free vs Premium builds.

Objective fixes
---------------
- command: Fixed objectives not progressing; commands are matched without leading slash (subcommands supported).
- placeholder: Fixed objectives not updating; polls PlaceholderAPI every second when installed.
- explore_biome: Biome matching uses registry keys (works on Paper 1.21+); supports names like plains, jungle, or JUNGLE.
- Progress path: Objectives that increment internally (fish, craft, etc.) no longer double-count on the standard event path.

Quest tracking
--------------
- Direct objectives (command, move, chat, vehicle, etc.) now respect active quest queue, conditions, and sequential order.
- Placeholder objectives use the same guards as other objectives (active paper, conditions, sequential).
- Queued (inactive) quest papers no longer gain progress until they become active.

Rewards & conditions
--------------------
- Command rewards support {player}, <player>, and %player% placeholders.
- completed-quests condition accepts a flat list in quest YAML.

Configuration & quests
----------------------
- quests.yml: 37 showcase_<type> quests for testing every objective type.
- quests.yml header documents all objective types and special fields.
- baker example uses harvest on wheat instead of break.
- config.yml references 37 objective types.

Download
--------
- Free and Premium from https://soapsuniverse.com and https://mythiccraft.io

Documentation
-------------
- All docs/ pages updated for 1.0.1 behavior, Free vs Premium jars, and troubleshooting.
- Published to: https://github.com/AlternativeSoap/SoapsQuest

Quality of life
---------------
- /sq reload reports how many quests loaded.
- /sq info shows Free or Premium edition and 37 objective types.
- Improved help and reload messages in messages.yml.

Requirements (unchanged)
--------------------------
- Paper 1.21+, Java 21+, SoapsCommon
- Optional: Vault, PlaceholderAPI, MythicMobs
